One-click solution for VNR VJIET Grievances

In the year 1995, Vignana Jyothi Society started the Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering &Technology (VNR VJIET) at Bachupally. Named after one of the donors and Vice Presidents of the Vignana Jyothi Society, VNRVJIET secured its affiliation to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University(JNTUH), Hyderabad and was established with the permission of The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Government of Andhra Pradesh. The Institute was accorded Autonomous status for Ten years by University Grants Commission (UGC) from the year 2018-19 to 2027-28. The institute is NAAC accredited with "A++" Grade under autonomous status.
